Plaintiff(s) FTA and Defendant(s) appeared and counter-claimed.
Plaintiff having failed to appear in court, the court finding a failure to prosecute the claim, the plaintiff's claim is dismissed prejudice. The court also finds no good cause for plaintiff’s failure to appear, and further finding notice of the defendant’s counterclaim and this hearing date was properly served on the plaintiff, the court finds the plaintiff in default on the counterclaim. The court has reviewed the submission of the defendant and considered defendant’s sworn testimony and finds there sufficient evidence of the validity of the defendant’s counterclaim.
